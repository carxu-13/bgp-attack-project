Detect suspicious BGP events where a specific service prefix is originated by an unexpected ASN in a way that appears intended to block or blackhole traffic rather than forward it normally. Build a baseline of expected origins for the service prefixes, then flag sudden origin changes for exact service prefixes or highly targeted announcements that deviate from the baseline. Increase confidence when the event is short-lived, highly specific, visible from external collectors, and inconsistent with the expected origin authorization state. Suppress alerts for approved origin migration, temporary maintenance, and benign measurement artifacts.
