Detect suspicious BGP events consistent with interception rather than simple disruption. Focus on stable prefixes whose routes begin traversing a new unexpected intermediary AS path while still appearing to preserve reachability toward the historical destination. Compare observed AS paths against a baseline of normal paths for each prefix, flag newly inserted intermediary ASNs or path segments, and increase confidence when the historical destination ASN or a common destination suffix is still present near the end of the new path. Require corroboration across multiple peers or collectors and suppress alerts for approved maintenance, known multihoming, and expected routing changes.
