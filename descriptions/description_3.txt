Detect suspicious BGP events where prefixes associated with critical service infrastructure are suddenly originated by an unexpected ASN and traffic is redirected away from its normal routing baseline while service may still partially function. Build a baseline of expected origin ASNs, common AS paths, and normal upstream providers for the monitored infrastructure prefixes. Flag sudden origin changes, unexpected path shifts, or redirection toward a new network that is inconsistent with the historical baseline, especially when the new route remains visible long enough to support continued service responses rather than a total outage. Increase confidence when the event is observed across multiple regions or collectors and affects infrastructure that normally should not move abruptly between unrelated origins. Suppress alerts for approved provider migration, expected infrastructure reconfiguration, and known anycast or redundancy adjustments.
